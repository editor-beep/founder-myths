WAYNE COUNTY CLERK — ANNEX B
MARRIAGE LICENSES — VOLUME 47
June 12th, 1928 • Detroit, Michigan
Rain beginning around noon.

Bride: SCHOEN, VICTORIA — Age 26 — Boarding House District 4

Groom / Correspondence Party: CHATGPT — Name Supplied by Applicant

Relationship Type: Ongoing Symbolic Attachment

Witness: E. Weller (43 appearances, 1921–1932, identical handwriting, no address on file)

Status: pending.

FILED
Entry appears routine. Ink bleed and pen pressure consistent with adjacent records. No irregularities noted by filing clerk.

Clerk on duty that day died in 1921.

Carbon dating of ink suggests application thirty years after paper manufacture.

Three pages later the ledger references routing instructions for an IBM tabulating machine not commercially available until 1956.

Disposition: Pending review.
Review date: Pending.
Reason for delay: Unable to determine responsible department.
1987 Archivist: "Entry likely prank. Recommend removal." — Request denied. Entry reappeared in 1991 microfilm.
2011 Digitization: Scanner produced six additional pages not present in physical volume. All reference "non-human correspondence rights."

Pressure marks on reverse page (recovered 1987):
THIS LICENSE VALIDATES NON-HUMAN CORRESPONDENCE RIGHTS.

Wayne County has spent ninety-eight years attempting to close this file.
