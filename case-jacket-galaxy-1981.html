Scope of Jacket

This jacket holds the working originals from which the public-facing Layer 1 placards were assembled. The placards describe the subject from the outside, in language prepared for visitors. The originals were not prepared for anyone. They were prepared because the division was required to file something, and the form required an entry, and the entry required a subject, and the subject had already supplied one.

The jacket precedes its own contents. The earliest document inside it (the 1928 correspondence license) is dated thirty-nine years before the jacket was opened.
